<p><p>The cutter stapler market is divided into two primary types: endo staplers and open staplers. Endo staplers are primarily used in minimally invasive surgeries, allowing for precise tissue manipulation and secure closure through small incisions. They offer benefits like reduced recovery times and lower infection risks. Open staplers, on the other hand, are utilized in traditional surgical procedures, providing reliable performance for larger incisions. Both types cater to varying surgical needs, enhancing patient outcomes across diverse medical settings.</p></p>
