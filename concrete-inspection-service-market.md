<p><strong>The Concrete Inspection Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Inspection</li><li>Manual Inspection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Concrete inspection services encompass various methods to evaluate the integrity of concrete structures. Ultrasonic inspection employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal defects such as cracks and voids, providing precise and non-destructive analysis. In contrast, manual inspection relies on visual assessment and traditional tools to identify surface imperfections and degradation. Each method serves distinct needs; ultrasonic inspection offers in-depth insights for complex structures, while manual inspection is efficient for straightforward assessments, ensuring overall safety and durability in construction.</p></p>
