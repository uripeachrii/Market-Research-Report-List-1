<p><strong>The Automotive Platooning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vehicle to Vehicle (V2V)</li><li>Vehicle to Infrastructure (V2I)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive platooning system market comprises two key types: Vehicle to Vehicle (V2V) and Vehicle to Infrastructure (V2I). V2V involves direct communication between vehicles to coordinate speed and movement, enhancing safety and fuel efficiency during group travel. In contrast, V2I connects vehicles to road infrastructure, allowing them to receive real-time data on traffic conditions and signals. Together, these systems improve traffic flow, reduce congestion, and optimize fuel consumption, paving the way for autonomous and connected vehicle technologies.</p></p>
