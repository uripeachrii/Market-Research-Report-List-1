<p><p>The ball bearing materials market comprises three primary types: steel, ceramic, and plastic. Steel ball bearings are known for their strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-load applications. Ceramic bearings offer superior hardness, low friction, and are resistant to corrosion, ideal for high-speed and high-temperature environments. Plastic bearings, while lighter and more resistant to chemicals, are typically used in applications where weight reduction and corrosion resistance are prioritized. Each material caters to specific performance needs in various industries.</p></p>
