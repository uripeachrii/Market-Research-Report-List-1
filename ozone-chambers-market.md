<p><strong>The Ozone Chambers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic Test</li><li>Static Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ozone Chambers market is categorized into dynamic and static testing types. Dynamic test chambers continuously circulate air, ensuring uniform ozone concentration and testing materials under flowing conditions, such as simulating real-world environmental exposure. In contrast, static test chambers maintain a stable environment with a fixed ozone level, ideal for evaluating the degradation of materials over time without airflow, providing insights into long-term resistance. Both types are crucial for assessing material performance against ozone degradation in various applications.</p></p>
