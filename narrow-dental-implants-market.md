<p><p>The Narrow Dental Implants Market features two primary types: root form and plate form dental implants. Root form implants are screw-shaped and designed to mimic the natural root structure of teeth, making them suitable for standard bone density cases. Conversely, plate form implants are flat and wide, specifically created for patients with limited bone width. Both types aim to provide stable support for artificial teeth, enhancing patient comfort and aesthetics while addressing varying bone conditions and dental requirements.</p></p>
