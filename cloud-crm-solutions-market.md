<p><p>Cloud CRM solutions are primarily categorized into two market types: SaaS (Software as a Service) or public cloud-based, and PaaS (Platform as a Service) or private cloud-based. SaaS solutions are hosted on external servers, allowing users to access CRM applications via the internet, offering scalability and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, PaaS solutions provide a customizable environment on private servers, granting businesses greater control over data security and configuration while supporting unique integration needs and specialized workflows.</p></p>
