<p><p>The Electromagnetic Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Transponder Market is divided into two main types: Low Frequency (LF) and High Frequency (HF). Low Frequency transponders operate typically around 125 kHz to 134 kHz, offering reliable performance over short distances and being suited for animal tracking and access control. In contrast, High Frequency transponders operate at 13.56 MHz, enabling faster data transfer rates and longer read ranges, making them ideal for applications like inventory management, payment systems, and contactless access control.</p></p>
