<p><p>The Smart Gas Detector market comprises two primary types: fixed gas detectors and portable gas detectors. Fixed gas detectors are permanently installed in specific locations, providing continuous monitoring of gas levels to ensure safety in industrial and commercial settings. They are ideal for detecting leaks over large areas. Portable gas detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices that offer flexibility for personal use or in varied environments, allowing for real-time monitoring and immediate assessment of gas hazards while on the move.</p></p>
