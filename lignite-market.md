<p><p>The lignite market is divided into two main types: xyloid lignite coal and compact lignite coal. Xyloid lignite, characterized by its woody texture, is often rich in organic materials and has a lower calorific value, making it suitable for specific applications like horticulture and bioenergy. In contrast, compact lignite is denser and has a higher calorific value, making it more efficient as a fuel source for power generation and industrial processes. Both types serve distinct industrial needs within the energy sector.</p></p>
