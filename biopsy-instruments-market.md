<p><p>The biopsy instruments market consists of various types, including core needle biopsy, aspiration biopsy, and vacuum-assisted biopsy. Core needle biopsy uses a hollow needle to extract a cylindrical tissue sample, allowing for accurate diagnosis. Aspiration biopsy employs a thin needle to withdraw fluid or cells, often used for less invasive sampling. Vacuum-assisted biopsy utilizes a suction device to collect larger tissue samples efficiently, providing comprehensive diagnostic information. Each method serves specific clinical needs, enhancing diagnostic accuracy in medical practice.</p></p>
