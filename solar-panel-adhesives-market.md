<p><strong>The Solar Panel Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epoxy</li><li>Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar panel adhesives market primarily consists of epoxy and polyurethane types. Epoxy adhesives offer strong bonding strength and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for the structural components of solar panels. In contrast, polyurethane adhesives provide flexibility and durability, allowing for expansion and contraction in varying temperatures, which is crucial for maintaining the integrity of solar installations. Both types are essential for ensuring the longevity and performance of solar energy systems, catering to different application needs in the industry.</p></p>
