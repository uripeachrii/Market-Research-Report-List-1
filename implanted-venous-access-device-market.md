<p><strong>The Implanted Venous Access Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PICC</li><li>Centrally Inserted Catheters (non-tunnelled and tunnelled)</li><li>Implanted Ports</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Implanted Venous Access Device market encompasses various types used for long-term medication administration and blood draws. PICC lines, or peripherally inserted central catheters, are inserted in a peripheral vein and extend to the central veins. Centrally inserted catheters are categorized into non-tunneled, inserted directly into the vein, and tunneled, which are placed beneath the skin. Implanted ports are surgically placed under the skin for easy access, while "others" may include specialized catheters for specific medical needs.</p></p>
