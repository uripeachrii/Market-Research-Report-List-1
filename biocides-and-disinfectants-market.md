<p><p>The biocides and disinfectants market is categorized into three main types: non-oxidizing chemicals, oxidizing chemicals, and others. Non-oxidizing chemicals, such as quaternary ammonium compounds, work by disrupting microbial cell membranes, providing effective control against bacteria and viruses. Oxidizing chemicals, like chlorine and hydrogen peroxide, kill pathogens through oxidative stress. "Others" include specialized products, such as natural biocides or proprietary formulations, catering to diverse applications across healthcare, agriculture, and food processing industries, addressing specific disinfection needs.</p></p>
