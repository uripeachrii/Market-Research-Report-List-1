<p><ul><li>Active Front Axle Steering</li><li>Active Rear Axle Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Active Steering System market is divided into two main types: Active Front Axle Steering and Active Rear Axle Steering. Active Front Axle Steering adjusts the angle of the front wheels in response to driving conditions, enhancing maneuverability and stability, particularly during sharp turns. Conversely, Active Rear Axle Steering alters the rear wheel angle, improving handling and reducing turning radius at higher speeds. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle control, safety, and overall driving experience by optimizing steering dynamics.</p></p>
