<p><strong>The Diathermy Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microwave Diathermy Equipment</li><li>Shortwave Diathermy Equipment</li><li>Ultrasound Diathermy Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diathermy equipment market is categorized into three main types: microwave diathermy, shortwave diathermy, and ultrasound diathermy. Microwave diathermy utilizes high-frequency microwaves to generate heat in tissues, often used for treating deep muscle pain. Shortwave diathermy employs high-frequency electromagnetic waves for deeper tissue heating, aiding in pain relief and inflammation reduction. Ultrasound diathermy uses sound waves to create thermal effects in soft tissues, promoting healing and reducing stiffness. Each type targets specific therapeutic needs in physical therapy and rehabilitation.</p></p>
