<p><p>The surface active agents market is categorized into four main types: anionic, cationic, zwitterionic, and non-ionic surfactants. Anionic surfactants, known for their excellent cleaning and foaming properties, are widely used in detergents. Cationic surfactants offer antimicrobial properties and are often found in fabric softeners and hair conditioners. Zwitterionic surfactants, which possess both positive and negative charges, are utilized in personal care products for their gentleness. Non-ionic surfactants are favored for their stability across various pH levels and are common in various industrial applications.</p></p>
