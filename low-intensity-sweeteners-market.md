<p><p>The low intensity sweeteners market features various sugar alternatives like xylitol, tagatose, allulose, trehalose, and isomaltulose. Allulose, a rare sugar, has negligible calories and mimics sugar's taste closely. Trehalose is known for its stability and potential health benefits, while isomaltulose is a slow-digesting sweetener that offers sustained energy.</p></p>
