<p><p>The Solid Bleached Sulfate (SBS) board market primarily consists of two types: SBS C1S (Coated One Side) and SBS C2S (Coated Two Sides). SBS C1S features a coated surface on one side, suitable for packaging applications where aesthetics are important, such as folding cartons and labels. In contrast, SBS C2S is coated on both sides, providing enhanced durability and print quality, making it ideal for more demanding applications like premium packaging and high-end graphic printing.</p></p>
