<p><p>The orthopedic bone cement market can be divided into four primary types based on viscosity and functionality. Low viscosity bone cements flow easily and are used for applications requiring precise placement. Medium viscosity cements offer a balance of workability and stability, suitable for standard orthopedic procedures. High viscosity cements provide enhanced stability and are preferred for weight-bearing applications. Antibiotic bone cements are infused with antimicrobial agents, offering infection control during surgical interventions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
