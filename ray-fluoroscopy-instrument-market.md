In contrast, Patient-Side Control Systems enable operators to interact directly with the imaging equipment at the patient's location, facilitating immediate adjustments during procedures. Both systems aim to improve diagnostic accuracy and patient comfort while streamlining workflow in medical settings.</p></p>
