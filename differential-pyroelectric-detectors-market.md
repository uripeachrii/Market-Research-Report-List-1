<p><p>Differential pyroelectric detectors are classified into two primary market types: single-channel and multi-channel detectors. Single-channel detectors are designed to measure temperature changes in a single point, making them suitable for applications requiring precise, direct temperature monitoring. In contrast, multi-channel detectors feature several sensing elements, allowing simultaneous detection across multiple points or areas. This versatility makes multi-channel detectors ideal for complex applications, such as environmental monitoring and industrial processes, where comprehensive spatial data is essential for accurate analysis.</p></p>
