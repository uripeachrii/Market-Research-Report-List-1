<p><strong>The Automotive Night Vision Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Far Infrared (FIR) Automotive Night Vision Sensors</li><li>Near Infrared (NIR) Automotive Night Vision Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive night vision sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: Far Infrared (FIR) and Near Infrared (NIR) sensors. FIR sensors detect heat emitted by objects, providing enhanced visibility in total darkness and detecting pedestrians or animals by their body heat. In contrast, NIR sensors utilize near-infrared light to illuminate the environment, allowing for clearer vision in low-light conditions. Both technologies improve driver safety by enhancing visibility during nighttime driving and adverse weather, contributing to advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).</p></p>
