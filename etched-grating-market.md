<p><ul><li>Transmission Grating</li><li>Reflection Grating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The etched grating market is segmented into two main types: transmission gratings and reflection gratings. Transmission gratings allow light to pass through, diffracting it into various wavelengths, making them ideal for applications like spectroscopy. Reflection gratings, on the other hand, reflect light off their surface for diffraction, suited for instrumentation and optical devices. Each type serves distinct applications in scientific research, telecommunications, and imaging technologies, catering to diverse industry needs and enhancing precision in optical performance.</p></p>
