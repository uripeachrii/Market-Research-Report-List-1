<p><p>The glass steel pipe market is segmented into three primary types based on resin composition: epoxy, polyester, and vinyl ester. Epoxy pipes offer superior mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making them suitable for demanding environments. Polyester pipes are more cost-effective and widely used in applications requiring moderate strength and corrosion resistance. Vinyl ester pipes combine the benefits of epoxy and polyester, providing enhanced resistance to corrosion and heat, ideal for industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
