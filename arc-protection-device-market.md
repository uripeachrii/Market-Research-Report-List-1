<p><p>The Arc Protection Device market consists of two main types: Busbar Type and Feeder Type. Busbar Type Arc Protection Devices are designed to monitor and protect busbars in electrical systems, ensuring rapid detection of arc faults to minimize damage. In contrast, Feeder Type Arc Protection Devices focus on safeguarding feeder circuits, detecting high-energy arcs specific to these lines. Both types enhance safety and reliability in electrical infrastructure by preventing equipment damage and reducing downtime through swift fault isolation.</p></p>
