<p><p>Separable tapered roller bearings are categorized into single row, double row, and four row types, each serving distinct applications. Single row bearings handle moderate loads and are commonly used in automotive and industrial machinery. Double row bearings offer increased load capacity and stability, suitable for heavy machinery. Four row bearings are designed for maximum load support and are ideal for extreme conditions, typically found in large complex setups like mining and construction equipment. Each type ensures efficient performance in various industries.</p></p>
