<p><p>The chemical tanker shipping market is categorized into three main types based on vessel size and operational scope. Inland chemical tankers (1,000-4,999 DWT) transport chemicals along rivers and lakes, focusing on regional distribution. Coastal chemical tankers (5,000-9,999 DWT) serve shorter maritime routes, linking ports within coastlines. Deep-sea chemical tankers (10,000-50,000 DWT) operate internationally, handling larger shipments over long distances. Each type plays a vital role in the efficient movement of chemical products across various geographical areas.</p></p>
